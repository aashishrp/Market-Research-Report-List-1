HDPE is a tough and rigid plastic known for its excellent chemical resistance and high strength-to-density ratio. LLDPE is a flexible plastic with good impact resistance and high tensile strength. LDPE is a flexible and lightweight plastic used for packaging applications due to its excellent moisture barrier properties. These three types of plastics cater to different industry needs and find diverse applications in sectors such as packaging, transportation, and construction.</p></p>

<p><p>The liquid packaging bags market comprises several types of materials, including PET, HDPE, PP, PVC, and Metalized Films. PP bags are made of polypropylene, offering great flexibility and moisture resistance. PVC bags are made of polyvinyl chloride, known for their excellent transparency and versatility. Metalized films bags are made by applying a thin layer of metal to films, providing excellent barrier properties against moisture and oxygen.</p></p>

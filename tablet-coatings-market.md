<p><p>Tablet coatings are applied to pharmaceutical tablets to provide various functionalities. Icing coatings offer a transparent, glossy appearance to tablets. Half or film coatings provide a thin layer for color, protection, or masking the taste. Enteric coatings prevent drug release in the stomach and ensure it's only released in the intestine. Slow release coatings allow the gradual release of drugs over time. Controlled release coatings release drugs based on specific conditions like pH or temperature. These coatings enhance the appearance, taste, protection, and controlled release of drugs in tablet form.</p></p>

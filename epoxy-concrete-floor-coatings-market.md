<p><p>Epoxy concrete floor coatings market includes three major types: water-based, solvent-based, and 100% solids. Water-based epoxy coatings are made of water as the primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-based epoxy coatings use solvents as the primary solvent, providing superior durability and adhesion properties. On the other hand, 100% solids epoxy coatings contain no solvents at all, offering maximum thickness and durability. All three types have their specific advantages and are used in various industries based on the requirements and preferences of the customers.</p></p>

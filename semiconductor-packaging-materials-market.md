The market types of semiconductor packaging materials include organic substrates, lead frames, bonding wires, and other materials. </p><p>Organic substrates are used as a base for mounting semiconductor chips and provide electrical insulation. Lead frames are metal frames that provide electrical connections between the semiconductor and external circuitry. Bonding wires are thin wires that connect the semiconductor chip to the lead frame. Other materials may include encapsulant materials, die attach materials, and solder balls used in the packaging process.</p></p>
